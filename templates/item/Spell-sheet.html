Spell Effect

The magician summons a number of lights that dance and whirl about the head. Each wisp provides light equivalent to a candle and are unaffected by wind and weather. They can even give light when submerged.

With a thought, the caster may send the lights down corridors or around corners, giving the appearance of a party of adventurers carrying candles. The caster may also command each light to hover in a different place or follow a different person. The magician may choose to end the spell at any time.

Materials:

A drop of pure honey smeared on the caster's lips
